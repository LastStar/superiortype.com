Vegan is a modern, structured sans featuring delicate, humanist elements. the distinctive shadow font Vega, which was created by Czech typographer Stanislav Maršo in the 1960s, was a source of inspiration for the designer.

The overall skeleton of the typeface is pleasantly optically narrowed and features a slight shadow. as a result of these characteristics, Vegan is a practical font that appears fresh and new. the font family contains six styles, from Light to Black and the corresponding italics. It can be used to set long texts, news papers and magazines, but is also perfect for large, striking headlines and posters.
